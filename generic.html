Web For Professionals

Web Development and Design for small businesses, freelancers and industry

Julius Goddard, CEO

I founded Web For Professionals in 2020 to provide web development services to large industrial businesses and local entrepreneurs.

Having worked in a variety of different fields such as industrial biotechnology, agriculture, entertainment and the arts myself, I founded Web For Professionals as a way to help small businesses build up a strong online presence.

After leaving my job as Health and Safety Officer at the biotech firm Ingenza in 2019, I decided to combine the knowledge I had picked up throughout my professional career and combine it with my existing knowledge of computers and networking to start my next career as a web developer.

Since then, I have devoted my time to working with small businesses, freelancers and industrial partners to develop websites and web applications.
